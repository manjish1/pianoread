# PianoRead

A mobile-first web app for practicing piano note reading. Quizzes you on bass and treble clef notes with instant feedback and daily progress tracking.

**Live app:** https://manjish1.github.io/pianoread/

## Features

- Bass clef (left hand) and treble clef (right hand) note reading
- Single notes and two-note chords
- Type your answer — no multiple choice
- Type `?` to skip and reveal the answer
- Instant correct/wrong feedback with a Next button to advance
- Daily score tracking (correct / wrong / skipped)
- Full history with mini staff notation thumbnails
- Reset today's score at any time
- Works fully offline after first load
- Installable as a home screen app on iPhone and Android

## Installing on iPhone

1. Open https://manjish1.github.io/pianoread/ in Safari
2. Tap the Share button (box with arrow at the bottom)
3. Tap **Add to Home Screen**
4. Tap **Add**

The app will appear on your home screen and open fullscreen without any browser chrome.

## How to play

- A staff with a note (or two) is shown
- Type the note letter (A–G) in the input box and tap **Check**
- For two-note chords, fill in both boxes
- Tap **Next →** to move to the next question
- Your daily stats are saved automatically

## Notes

- Natural notes only (A–G, no sharps or flats)
- 70% bass clef, 30% treble clef
- All data is stored locally on your device — nothing is sent anywhere
